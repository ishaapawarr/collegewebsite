ACADEMICS

Computer Engineering
CMPN

Computer engineering combines hardware and software aspects to design and create computer systems, from microprocessors to software applications.

Electronics & Computer Science
ECS

ECS blends the principles of electronic engineering and computer science to create advanced systems, emphasizing hardware-software integration.

Electrical Enginnering
ELEC

EE encompasses the study of electrical systems, including power generation, distribution, and electrical devices, critical for powering our homes, industries, and technology.

Information Technology
IT

IT focuses on the management and application of technology for data storage, retrieval, and communication within organizations, enabling efficient business processes and decision-making.

Electronics & Telecommunication
EXTC

EXTC concentrates on electronic systems, telecommunication networks, and data transmission, enabling modern communication and information exchange.

Humanities & Applied Sciences
HAS

HAS focuses on the development of both hardware components (e.g., circuits) and software applications, ensuring seamless interaction and functionality in computing systems.
