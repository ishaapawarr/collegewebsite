An Electronics and Computer Science Department is a crucial academic unit within a university or educational institution that focuses on teaching, research, and innovation in the fields of electronics engineering and computer science. Electronics and computer science departments typically have well-equipped laboratories for hands-on learning and research. These labs may focus on digital electronics, microelectronics, computer programming, and networking. Specialized facilities may include clean rooms for semiconductor fabrication, robotics labs, and computer clusters for high-performance computing.

Welcome to the Department of Electronics & Computer Science at Atharva College of Engineering (ACE), Mumbai. The department is located in a capacious environment with state of art facilities & highly qualified faculty. Electronics & Computer Science is an ever evolving field that has benefitted the progress of society. In the 21st Century, Electronics Industry has made a tremendous impact on our lives. Electronic gadgets have become an integral part of our lifestyle. In today’s fast-changing world, a new generation of Electronics Engineers needs to be developed to provide environment friendly solutions to the challenges faced due to limited resources. The department has a dedicated faculty to develop all round Electronics & Computer Science graduates with highly professional & Ethical values. The department works with the objective of addressing the challenges faced by the Academia, Industry & the Society. The department endeavors to create a conducive for the students to flourish and aid them in their overall growth, making them ready to be successful and competent in the real world. I, Prof. Kavita Piyush Bani, (I/N) Head of the Department, once again welcome you to the Department of Electronics Engineering as an undergraduate student & we would be delighted to be a part of your journey. The department is always ready to receive valuable inputs to aid us to propagate & hence is open to all Stakeholders’ to be part of our growth.
